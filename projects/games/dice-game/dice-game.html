Game Rules

Welcome to the Dice Game!

This can be a fun game!
How to Play:
You can either play this game as one person rolling the dice for both sides, or with a friend. The object is to get a total of 50 points. Player 1 clicks on "Roll dice" to get the game started. When it is your turn, you can keep clicking on "Roll dice" but beware! If you roll a 1, your "Current" points will return to 0 and then it is the next Player's turn. When you feel you have clicked on the "Roll dice" enough, click on the "Hold" button and your current points will move to your Total points. Then, it will be the next Player's turn. First Player to 50, wins!
